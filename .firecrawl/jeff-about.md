# About Us

## Hi, I’m Jeff

![](https://freemindcreativeenergy.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/ChatGPT-Image-Mar-31-2025-08_47_09-PM.png)

I am the connector to the Source. The Source is the origin of everything and when we are connected to it, we are one in the present, aware, active, joyful, whole, seen and free.

With the Source, we are creators. We have the ability to create our realities and lives as we want it.

I help to awaken the source and creator in you through my mastery workshops, energy cards, coaching and Freemind Source energy art.

About my background, I am an artist, trained as an architect and was a director of a design school, who has practiced art and design for the last 20 years.

I have a Bachelors of Degree in Architecture from the National University of Singapore, and a Masters in History & Theory of Architecture from McGill University, Montreal Canada. My Creative Energy Art Works have been exhibited and collected in Singapore, Hong Kong, Paris and Seoul.

I studied mindfulness with a master in the course of my art practice and teaching art & design students towards original creation, I discovered a convergence of my mindfulness practice with Art Making .

I discovered that to be able to create truly original works, there is a need to access the creative source. Inspired by what many great artists and composers have said that their great works flow through them and they were just a channel, and to be able to access the creative source in a deliberate way (not accidental). I discovered one can do it through stilling the mind by drawing as mindfulness-as-action.

I then went on to create the FreeMind Art and Drawing Energy Huaqi process 20 years ago, sharing this process with my students.

It is a focused drawing as mindfulness-in-action process that regulates and boosts positive energy, taps on the creative source within all of us, to enable students towards self-realisation and to create their own unique style of art.

My students consist of the young to seniors for originality, creativity, mindfulness, and de-stressing, as well as coaching creatives and artists to Unleash your originality and creativity, to develop your careers and enterprises, while on your journey to self-realization and purpose.
