# Video Interview Questions

---

## Video 1 — The Why

- Why did you create this workshop?
- What kind of person is this workshop for?
- What problem does this workshop solve?
- What does it feel like to be disconnected from your body?
- What do you want people to feel by the end of a session?

---

## Video 2 — What Happens in a Session

- What actually happens inside a session, step by step?
- Why do you use movement, breathwork, and stillness together?
- What should someone expect if it's their first time?
- Do people need any experience to join?
- What do people usually say after their first session?

---

## Video 3 — About the Coach

- Can you introduce yourself and what you do?
- What led you to this work?
- What is the one thing you want every participant to take home?
- Who is this workshop really for?
- What would you say to someone who is hesitant to join?

---

## Video 4 — Participant Testimonial

- Before the workshop, what were you feeling or struggling with?
- What was your experience during the session?
- What changed for you after the workshop?
- Who would you recommend this to?
